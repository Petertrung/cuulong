History

At Cuu Long we work long and hard to create a good experience for our customers. This in turn creates long-lasting relationships with our customers. This has been the root and tradition that we have stood by since the start of this company. A tested and true model that has brought us the great support and customer base that we have today. We started this company in 1994 seeing that there was a need for a meat distributor with the growing amount of amazing Vietnamese restaurants and the popularity of these restaurants at an all-time high they needed to spend more time cooking and prepaing the food while we delivered great quality and cheap meats. Since then we have grown through word of mouth and now provide other items like fish sauce, fish, vegetables, and much much more. We would like you to join our long tradition of quality service and join the Cuu Long family.
